Personalization/Customization

Personalization consists of tailoring a service or a product to accommodate specific individuals, sometimes tied to groups or segments of individuals. And it is a great way to know your employee or your clients. It helps to keep them stay & engaged with your brand for longer than expected.

Embossing

Embossing and debossing are the processes of creating either raised or recessed relief images and designs in paper and other materials & personalizing the details of the company Logo, Name, Tag Line etc.

Sublimation & Printing

Sublimation is the transition of a substance directly from the solid to the gas state, without passing through the liquid state on the products & Printing is a process for mass reproducing text and images using a master form or template etc.

Embroidery & More..

Embroidery is the craft of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn. Embroidery may also incorporate other materials such as pearls, beads, quills, and sequins.
